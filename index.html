I am a software engineer from Bangladesh, working as a freelancer.

My journey starts from a small village named Machhimpur. I was born in this village and then at the age of 4, I migrated to a nearby town called Gouripur. I have spent almost all my life in gouripur. Later in my life, I had to go to Dhaka to parsue my college and university education. I love travelling, fishing and playing football. Doing scientific experiment is one of my favourite hobbies. I really like to playing with chemicals and try to make some handicrafts.

I had enjoyed an adventurous journey in terms of my schooling. At the early stage of my life, I admitted in a pre-primary school named "Ikra kindergarten" which was in Narandia. Then I went to Gouripur and joined a renowned school called "Ananda Multimedia School"; however, I did not able to continue for long time. My parents always want me to sit on government scholarship exam. So, I left "AMS" and started going "Gouripur Government Primary school". Interesting fact is, I left "GGPS" within 4 months since my parents went back to my village Machhimpur. In my village, I got admitted into "Machhimpur Government Primary school" and successfully passed PSC exam with "government scholarship". After that I admitted into "Machhimpur R R Instituion". Again, I came back to Gouripur and joined "Gouripur S A High School". I passed my SSC from "GSAHS" and went to Dhaka to attend college. My college was "Dhaka City College". Finally, completing my college, I got admitted to "Daffodil International University" and achieved my "B.Sc" degree in the field of "Computer Science". Now, I am plannig to go abroad for my "M.Sc".
